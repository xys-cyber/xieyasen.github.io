2. 法庭意见书要素

Key Elements of Court Opinions

  • Case Citation: Lochner v. New York, 198 U.S. 45 (1905)
    案件引用格式：当事人 v. 当事人, 卷号 U.S. 页码 (年份)
  • Procedural History: Error to the County Court of Oneida County, State of New York
    程序历史：从纽约州奥奈达县法院提起的错误审查
  • Facts: Lochner convicted of violating New York labor law by permitting employee to work more than 60 hours per week
    案件事实：Lochner因允许雇员每周工作超过60小时而违反纽约州劳动法被定罪
  • Legal Issue: Whether New York's maximum hours law violated the Fourteenth Amendment's Due Process Clause
    法律争议：纽约州最高工时法是否违反第十四修正案的正当程序条款
  • Holding: Yes, the law violated the freedom of contract protected by the Fourteenth Amendment
    判决结果：是的，该法律违反了第十四修正案保护的合同自由
  • Reasoning: The right to contract is part of liberty protected by the Fourteenth Amendment; baking is not an inherently unhealthy occupation
    判决理由：合同权是第十四修正案保护自由的一部分；烘焙并非固有有害健康的职业

Constitutional Principles

This case established the "Lochner Era" doctrine, where the Court strictly scrutinized economic regulations under substantive due process. The decision emphasized:

  • Freedom of contract as a fundamental right
  • Limited scope of police power
  • Judicial review of economic legislation
